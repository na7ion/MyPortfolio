About Me

Hi, I'm Zymon, 22 years old, from Olongapo City. I graduated from CELTECH College Olongapo City with a passion for data analysis. I chose to become a data analyst because I love data, particularly statistics. Growing up, whenever I played games, I was obsessed with looking at my stats, comparing them to other players' stats, user stats, and team stats. I loved asking myself questions like HOW are we winning at a certain time, WHAT do I need to do to perform better, and WHY is there a trend after a patch or an update. I think that kind of obsession was the catalyst for my choice to become a data analyst.

I enjoy looking at well-visualized statistics, and I'm at a point in my life where I am asking myself how I can do that professionally.
